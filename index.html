Welcome to Grundy's Grotto!

About us:

Here at Grundy's Grotto, we aim to provide a sense of blissful relaxation blened with beautiful fairtrade coffee with elegant flavours, syrups and pastries to meet all your self-care needs! We aim to make a comfortable enviroment for more introverted people with cosy corners and safe areas where you can cosy up, enjoy the beautiful brown, and melt away...

That being said, we invite all to come and give the Grundy's Grotto a fair try as we believe there is simply nothing better than a nicely lit, rustic wooden shop where you can have the finest pastries to go with your coffee, and thats the Grundy Guarentee!
